Keshi: Main Character Energy

Album: always
Song: drunk
by Vanessa Leung
Jan 8, 2022

Keshi’s song “drunk” is an embodiment of the first year uni experience — everyone’s drunk, you just want to go to bed, and at the end of the day you’re just freaking lonely. It’s the song to listen to when you’re experiencing FOMO, manifesting your “main character energy” at a hipster café, or literally when all your friends are drunk again. Keshi’s signature “lo-fi” sound gets us “in our feels” with his smooth vocals and laissez-faire style of singing.

Soulja Boy: STAN.

Album: iSouljaBoyTellem
Song: Kiss Me Thru The Phone
by Grace Suh
Jan 8, 2022

Before Soulja's claim to fame as the SouljaGame console mogul, he was a key definer of the mid to late 2000s music zeitgeist. Despite his slightly delayed — even off-beating rapping at times — and simplistic wordplay, his infectious and boisterous confidence radiates from each song and compensates for any deficiencies in the lyrical department. Kiss Me Thru The Phone enraptures and sends the listener back to a time of flip phones and true religion jeans. With the genius addition of Sammi to sing the iconic chrous and line of "six seven eight triple nine eight two one two", Kiss Me Thru The Phone appeals to the universal sensation of youth and has stood the test of time.

Dua Lipa: Main Character Energy

Album: Future Nostalgia
Song: Love Again
by Saryu Rawaley
Jan 8, 2022

British Pop Icon Dua Lipa won Pop vocal album of the year at the last Grammys.Her album Future Nostalgia is pop-funk with disco and electronic influences to pay homage to the iconic 80’s and 90’s pop culture scene. This no skip album made huge waves across popular social media services like Tiktok, as the catchy beats and lyrics made everyone in their feels. Popular song Love Again is the perfect hit for a party playlist or just to jam out to on your own. The song Love again has done tremendously well on the billboard charts and Dua’s tone and artistry really shines through. A music video was made for this song which featured Dua in an interesting “cowboy chic” sort of look, which definitely was a main character moment.
